Game Instructions

Open tiles by clicking on them. Right click tiles to flag and un-flag them as mines. If you mark a tile as a mine incorrectly, you will have to correct the mistake before you can win. When you're done, click validate to see if you avoided trouble. With cheats on, posers can hover over tiles to get hints.
